# Privacy Policy for Netwrk

**Last Updated: January 4, 2026**

## Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Netwrk ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
We collect your device's location to:
- Show nearby contacts on the map
- Help you discover people in your area
- Your location is only collected while the app is in use and is not tracked in the background

### Camera and Photos
We request access to your camera and photo library to:
- Allow you to take or select profile pictures for your contacts
- Allow you to take or select profile pictures for your own account
- We do not access your camera or photos without your explicit permission
- Photos are only accessed when you choose to add or change a profile picture

### Contacts
We request access to your contacts to:
- Allow you to import your existing contacts into Netwrk
- We only access contacts with your explicit permission
- You control which contacts are imported into the app

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:
- Provide core functionality of the app (contact management, location-based features)
- Improve user experience
- Display your contacts and their information within the app

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely on our servers
- We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information
- We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:
- **Google Maps**: For displaying maps and location-based features. Google Maps has its own privacy policy available at https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request deletion of your personal information
- Withdraw permission for location, camera, or contacts access at any time through your device settings
- Request correction of inaccurate information

## Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Permissions Summary

The app requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose | When Collected |
|------------|---------|----------------|
| Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION) | Show nearby contacts on map | While app is in use |
| Camera | Take profile pictures | Only when you choose to take a photo |
| Photo Library | Select profile pictures | Only when you choose to select a photo |
| Contacts | Import contacts into app | Only when you choose to import |

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not track your location in the background
- We do not access your camera or photos without explicit action
- We do not share your location with other users without your permission
- We do not sell your data to advertisers or third parties
